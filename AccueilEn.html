Training today those who will advance science, education, and health tomorrow.

Coop in vivo is a non-profit organization that promotes hands-on training in biotechnology in developing countries. We collaborate with local institutions and pharmaceutical companies to strengthen scientific autonomy and improve access to healthcare tools for everyone.

Why does our mission matter?

Practical training allows students to acquire concrete, professional skills.

In many countries, these opportunities are limited or nonexistent.

Coop in vivo works to ensure that access to science and healthcare tools is not dependent on place of birth.
