Our projects

Francisco Paula University of Santander – Cúcuta, Colombia
We collaborate with the Biotechnology Engineering program to develop the health component of their curriculum. Our activities include: • Hosting students in Belgium for their final-year projects • Training faculty members in molecular biology, cell culture, and virology • Assisting in the implementation of laboratory practicals • Providing scientific equipment adapted to local needs.

University of Antioquia – Medellín, Colombia
We are launching a collaboration on online courses in the field of biopharmaceuticals, with a catalog of courses in French and Spanish. A pilot project is currently being developed.

Hope Foundation Primary & High School – Bangalore, India
In partnership with a local school, we are initiating a collaboration to develop remote microbiology workshops, using equipment already installed on-site. Objective: To raise students' awareness of biology and the importance of hygiene, while sparking their scientific curiosity.

University of Tlemcen – Algeria
In November 2025, a lecturer-researcher will come to our partner center for training in cell culture and cytotoxicity. This training will strengthen her research on medicinal plants and help her develop new practical projects in Tlemcen.
